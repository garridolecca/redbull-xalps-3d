Map Layers Explained

  • Glowing Lines = athlete GPS tracks at actual altitude
  • Colored Dots = athlete current position (animated)
  • Gold/Red 3D Cylinders = thermal lift detected (taller = stronger climb)
  • Blue 3D Cones = wind direction & speed (bigger = stronger wind)
